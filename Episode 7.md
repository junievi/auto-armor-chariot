# Episode 3

Cut to [[Ommatokoita Ancylos Lichtenberg|Omma]] and [[Jesse Anderson|Andi]], fighting the Nimrod Revenant in the thick, tropical jungle below the ship. It's hiiiiigh noooon. Nimrod fires at Omma, who runs off in the direction the shot came from. She finds an artillery cannon pointed at her former position. There appears to be some sort of "radio device" attached to the cannon, which catches her attention. What she doesn't see is the tripwire at her feet. Cut back to Andi, who gazes in shock at the resulting explosion.

Cut to the bridge. Mister Jack runs to the radio and starts shouting into it

> Omma and Andi you better kill that sonofabitch!

The bridge freezes, and Isaac coolly responds
> Mister Jack, do you have any pertinent intelligence to share that may be of use to our channellers?

In the Captain's ready room, he waits silently for her report. Before Jack can explain, another cannon blast hits the ship. 

> He was a gallowsman. a mercenary . a sniper. nobody knew what he or his mech looked like, but he's the one who wiped out my squad. He's supposed to be dead, I shot him myself. 

Isaac stands and walks back to the bridge, ordering Mister Jack to relay any tactical information she knows to Omma and Andi

> And Mister Jack? We're going to get him this time. For good.

Cut to Engineering. As medics and repair crews (accompanied by [[Leo Socorrin]]) jostle to get through the door, Yuri surreptitiously conceals a [[listening device]].  He whispers to Doc to be on the lookout for the saboteur to return and check their work. Doc nods and sets about ordering the repair crews to replace the boiler.  Leo starts trying to move the injured to sickbay, but Doc reminds him that transporting unstable patients will likely kill them. Sensing his uncertainty, Yuri puts a hand on the side of his arm and gives him some encouraging words

> > I think you can do it. Do you think you can give it a shot?
>
> > You're right. I can do this. I _can_ do this 

Surprised by how well Yuri calmed the young surgeon, Doc says "You're more competent than I thought!"
